## ğŸ® What is Looters Land?

**Looters Land** is a **gacha-style idle RPG** where you:
- **Collect 60+ unique heroes** through gacha summons
- **Form a party of 4 heroes** for turn-based 4v4 battles
- **Explore a procedural 50x50 worldmap** with fog of war
- **Delve into infinite dungeons** with 10 different room types
- **Compete on daily leaderboards** for rewards
- **Hunt rare bosses** for legendary loot

**Play Time:** 30-60 minutes per day (energy-based progression)

## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How do I get more heroes?
**A:** Use **Gacha Summons** in the Tavern:
- **Daily free summon:** 1x per day (resets at midnight)
- **Gold summons:** 1,000g (1x) or 9,000g (10x)
- **Duplicates merge** for talent points (+5% stats)

### Q: What should I do with duplicate heroes?
**A:** **Keep them!** Duplicates automatically merge and give:
- **+1 Talent Point** per duplicate
- **Max 6 talent points** per hero
- **+5% all stats** per talent point
- **Total:** +30% all stats at max talents

### Q: How do I heal my heroes?
**A:** Currently (v0.7.3):
- **Rest Rooms** in dungeons (heal 10% HP)
- **Cleric skills** (Heal, Group Heal)
- **Paladin skills** (Righteous Strike)
- **Coming v0.9.0:** Healer building in towns

### Q: What's the best way to spend energy?
**A:** Depends on your goal:
- **XP/Leveling:** Dungeons (10 energy per floor, guaranteed combat)
- **Gold:** Worldmap encounters (5 energy, fast fights)
- **Loot:** Dungeon bosses (every 5 floors, guaranteed epic)
- **Legendary items:** Rare bosses (5 energy, 24h cooldown)

### Q: Should I enchant all my items?
**A:** **No!** Only enchant:
- **Rare+ rarity** items (blue, purple, orange borders)
- **Items you'll use long-term** (don't enchant Common)
- **Start with +1 to +5** (cheap, high success rate)
- **+6 to +10** is expensive (save for endgame)

### Q: How does the pity system work?
**A:** After **100 summons** without getting Epic or Legendary:
- Next summon is **guaranteed Epic or better**
- Counter resets when you get Epic/Legendary
- **Tracks across sessions** (saved in database)

### Q: Can I play offline?
**A:** No, **Looters Land requires internet connection**:
- Real-time multiplayer (see other players)
- Cloud saves (auto-save every 2 seconds)
- Global weather/time synchronization
- Leaderboards

### Q: What happens if I die in combat?
**A:** **No death penalty!**
- Heroes drop to **10% HP**
- You keep **all items and gold**
- In dungeons: Auto-exit to worldmap
- Can heal at Rest Rooms or use Cleric

### Q: How do leaderboards reset?
**A:** **Daily at 00:00 UTC** (coming in v0.8.0):
- New rankings start
- Previous day rewards distributed
- New worldmap seed generates
- Energy resets to 240
